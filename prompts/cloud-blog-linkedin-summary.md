🚀 You can deploy a full app stack to production for $0/month — if you know where to look.

Body:
Most developers assume cloud deployment means a monthly bill. Azure, AWS, and GCP pricing pages don't make it easy to find otherwise.

But all three maintain genuinely always-free tiers — not 90-day trials, not credits — that are generous enough to run a real production app at low-to-moderate traffic.

I mapped the free limits across every layer of a typical full-stack app:
✅ Static frontend hosting
✅ Serverless backend functions
✅ Virtual machines (OCI is the hidden gem here)
✅ Containers + registries
✅ CI/CD pipelines
✅ SQL & NoSQL databases
✅ ML inference & data analytics

Plus the cheapest paid option for layers where free runs out — so you know your ring to a stack.
