About Me

I am a Full-Stack Software Architect focused on designing and delivering scalable, resilient, cloud-native systems across AWS, GCP, and Azure. My work spans the full lifecycle — from architecture and design through implementation, deployment, and operation at scale.

With an M.Tech in Data Science, I work at the intersection of enterprise software architecture and modern data systems. My current focus and ongoing research center on:

  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (AI/ML)
  • Generative AI (GenAI) & Large Language Models (LLMs)
  • Scalable Distributed Systems & Multi-Cloud Architecture

What to Expect

I publish technical articles, architectural guides, and research notes grounded in production experience:

  1. System Design & Cloud Architecture: Proven patterns and trade-off analysis for building multi-cloud solutions that scale.
  2. AI & Data Engineering: In-depth treatments of machine learning workflows, data pipeline engineering, and applied GenAI.
  3. Modern Web & Full-Stack Development: Practical guidance and best practices for building robust, maintainable applications.

Expect rigor over hype and depth over breadth. The first articles are on the way.
